Tips :


Trade only between 9:30–11:30 AM and 1:30–3:00 PM

These are the highest-volume, highest-volatility windows with the cleanest setups. The 11:30 AM–1:30 PM window is typically low volume, choppy, and full of false breakouts — a trap for active traders.
Example: You get a "perfect looking" breakout at 12:15 PM. Volume is half the morning average. Skip it. The same setup at 10:00 AM with 2× volume is worth taking.


ORB setup or box theory :

Skip if breakout happens after 10:30 AM
ORB setups lose their edge as the morning progresses. A "breakout" at 11 AM is not an ORB trade — it is a different setup entirely. ORB only works in the first 60–90 minutes.
